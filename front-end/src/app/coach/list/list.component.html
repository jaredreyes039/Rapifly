Quick Start Guide

Lost? Overwhelmed? Let's ease the stress of learning a new piece software so your team can spend time on what matters to you. Below are guides for each vital function of Rapifly's unique methodology; simply select any item that you need assistance with, and if the guide is not clear enough, feel free to explore the FAQ's, reach out to an expert (coming soon...), or file a bug report.

  • The Hud

    {{activeStage}}

    The first step in the CEE methodology, CREATE is the stage for all things planning and preparing. Getting started is a simple process:

    1. Under CREATE, create your first root item if you haven't already; this will set your target goals, plan dates, and more for all sub-plans and modules. For more on sub-plans and modules, check out their respective guides below.
    2. Once you've created the parent plan, your next step is to enter the Brainstorm phase and delve deep into the simple, yet powerful tools Rapifly has to offer!

    {{activePhase}}

    The BRAINSTORM phase is the most crucial part of the CREATE stage, allowing you to attach sub-plans and modules to any root plan, and nest those plan items as necessary. To get the most out of the BRAINSTORM phase, we suggest creating at least 1-3 sub-plans, and exploring the various modules and challenges available for attachment. Ultimately, the HUD is your source for planning your goals, so the route that works with your goals is the best route.

    {{activePhase}}

    The PRIORITIZE tool allows you to quickly organize the prioritization of the various items that form your root plan, and giving you the power to focus on what matters most.

    Using the PRIORITIZE phase is incredibly simple: all plans are assigned a base priority number, and with the higest priority closest to 0. This means your top priority will always be top of the list, and vice versa, allowing for seemless transition between plans based on when you need to focus on them.

    {{activePhase}}

    The DEACTIVATE phase is a tool for determining what plan items should be actively pursued, and which can be set aside. Ultimately, any item that is deactivated is only such until you choose to reactivate the item, if at all. You are a busy individual, and you deserve the most efficient tools for quickly filing through your plans- DEACTIVATE is here to help.

    Using the DEACTIVATE phase is as easy as clicking the deactivate button for any particular sub-item under your root plan. Any deactivated sub-items with child items of their own will deactivate ALL child items as well.

    NOTE: Deactivated items will not be usuable in any following phases until they are reactivated, and ultimately have no impact on any measurements and summaries made.

    {{activePhase}}

    Looking to bring attention to a particular plan item? Working on a large team where ideas tend to get lost. Introducing the PROPOSE phase, a tool dedicated to giving each user in your team a voice. With the ability to propose any plan item, your entire team has the chance to bring their plans and ideas to the forefront, allowing for voting in the next phase.

    Individual user? No problem, the PROPOSE phase gives you the opportunity to finalize the plans you want to proceed further with, bringing them to a vote for comparison with each other.

    {{activePhase}}

    Introducting one of the most revolutionary tools behind Rapifly's CEE methodology- the VOTE phase- a tool that brings a democratic approach to the team planning process. Now, all members on your team have a chance to vote on the plans that matter most, allowing for a better projection of where you want to take your goals next, and how those goals will be pursued.

    Here at Rapifly, we understand how important it is for all members on a team to have a say in key planning decisions. As teams grow, more and more ideas are proposed, and the VOTE phase will be the final disseminating factor for making the best planning decisions possible.

    Not working with a team? You're in luck, while voting may not necessarily democratize your solo projects, it provides you a chance to reflect and determine for yourself which plans you think are the most effective. Never before has the decision making involved in planning been so simple, so efficient, and so user-friendly- welcome to the power of Rapifly!

    {{activePhase}}

    This is it- the final moment before execution- welcome to the SELECT phase. Following a thorough vote across your various plan items, all plan items will appear with their respective vote counts, and all that is left to do is toggle which plans you want to execute.

    SELECT is the final phase of the CREATE stage, and notably the most important- how do you determine which plans are most worth pursuing?

    1. Which plans have the highest and lowest vote counts? Typically, we recommend pursuing plans with the most positive overall vote.
    2. If plans have tied vote counts, which had the higher priority? Perhaps this is a quick reference that can guide your final decision.
    3. If there are few to no plans with positive votes, weigh your options carefully, and consider circling back with yourself or your team to determine a better course of action.

    {{activeStage}}

    Welcome to the EXECUTE stage, the second stage in Rapifly's unique CEE methodology. Under EXECUTE, you will find tools for delegating active plans, tracking the countdown until your plan is set to launch, and launch your plans for evaluation and improvement!

    NOTE: The DELEGATE tool is currently under contstruction as we expand to more users. Check back in often for updates!
